About

The Photographer

Luz Hernandez Kroll

Wildlife & Nature Photographer

Based in the rugged wilderness of the American West, Luz Hernandez Kroll is a wildlife photographer whose work takes her into the heart of some of North America's most breathtaking landscapes — from the High Uintas of Utah to the alpine meadows of the Rocky Mountains.

With a Canon in hand and an intimate knowledge of wildlife behavior, Luz seeks out the quiet, unguarded moments that reveal the true character of each animal. Her photography is driven by a deep love for the natural world and a commitment to sharing its beauty with others.
